NmeaGGAFix

NmeaGGAFix class

NmeaGGAFix includes fix quality of a GPS which is obtained from the GGA message. More information on GGA messages here https://receiverhelp.trimble.com/alloy-gnss/en-us/NMEA-0183messages_GGA.html Generally a fix of 1 or 2 lends to large position errors, ideally we want a fix of 4-5. Other fixes are unsuitable for outdoor navigation. The meaning of each fix value is documented here https://docs.novatel.com/OEM7/Content/Logs/GPGGA.htm#GPSQualityIndicators
